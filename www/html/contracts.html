1. How to create new smart-contract?

To create a smart contract, there are 2 ways: simple and difficult. A simple way is to go to the "Smart Contract's Templates" section and select one of the proposed templates to create your own smart contract, if the template matching your list is not available, you can go to the "Standart Smart Contracts" section and use the smart code editor contracts to create the smart contract that you wish.

2. How to overview all current smart-contracts and it's operations?

To see a complete list of your smart contracts, you must go to the "My Smart Contracts" section, from this section you can see the list of all your smart contracts, as well as their current status. To view the statistics of your smart contracts you need to go to the Grafana system.
